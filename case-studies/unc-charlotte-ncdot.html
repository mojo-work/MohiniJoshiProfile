Transportation Data Quality & KPI Dashboards

I partnered with stakeholders to resolve data mismatches and deliver interactive Power BI dashboards that made KPI reporting faster and more consistent.

Outcome
45% reduction in manual reporting time
Focus
Data quality · KPI visibility
Tools
Power BI · Excel · MS Office
Operating model
Cross-team coordination

Problem

Stakeholders needed clear, reliable KPI reporting, but data mismatches and inconsistent inputs created rework and slowed down updates.

What I did

  • Coordinated with internal and external stakeholders to resolve data mismatches and form inconsistencies.
  • Tracked issue resolution and status updates using MS Office tools and G-chart dashboards.
  • Designed and developed interactive Power BI dashboards to visualize KPIs and support data-driven decisions.

Outputs & impact

  • Delivered dashboards that standardized KPI views and improved reporting consistency.
  • Reduced manual reporting effort by 45% through interactive Power BI visuals.
  • Improved stakeholder confidence by resolving mismatches and documenting assumptions.

What I’d do next

  • Add automated data quality checks (validation rules + exception reporting) before refresh cycles.
  • Define KPI ownership and refresh SLAs to keep reporting predictable.
  • Expand dashboard self-serve with role-based views for different stakeholder needs.
